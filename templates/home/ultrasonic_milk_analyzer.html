Ultrasonic Milk Analyzer

Download Pdf

Introducing our Ultrasonic Milk Analyzer: Compact, reliable, and cost-effective. Ideal for stationary and mobile setups, it accurately analyzes milk components, enhancing productivity.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

  • Brand: RTPL
  • Model: ANALYZER RTPL
  • Display Type: 128x64 Blue color Graphics LCD
  • Operating Voltage: 12 Volt DC
  • Operating Temperature: 5 to 60 Degree °C
  • Relative Humidity: 30 to 80 %
  • Power Supply: Adaptor 230VAC To 12VDC / 3A
  • Power consumption: 30 W
  • Peak Power:60 W
  • Net Weight: 2.3 Kg

Technical Specification

  • Direct Measuring Parameters:
  • 1. FAT: 0.1% to 15% with accuracy ±0.1%
  • 2. SNF: 0.1% to 15% with accuracy ±0.1%
  • 3. Temperature : 5°C to 45°C
  • Calculated Parameters:
  • 1. CLR: 20 to 40 with accuracy ±0.5
  • 2. Protein: 2% to 7% with accuracy ±0.2
  • 3. Lactose: 0.01% to 10% with accuracy ±0.2
  • 4. Added water in Milk: 0% to 99% with accuracy ±1%
  • Resolution:
  • 1. FAT, SNF, Lactose, Protein, Salts: 0.1%
  • 2. Temperature : 1°C
  • 3. CLR: 0.5
  • 4. Added Water: 1%
  • Measurement Time:
  • 1. 20 Sec @ 28°C
  • Cabinet:
  • 1. 304 Industrial grade Stainless Steel
